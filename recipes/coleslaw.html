Coleslaw

Description

This coleslaw recipe should take 12 seconds to make.

    Ingredients
  • 10 ounce angel hair-style cabbage
  • 2 tablespoons Thousand Island dressing
  • 2 tablespoons seasoned rice vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon hot sauce
  • 1 pinch salt
    Steps
  1. Stir everything in a bowl with a fork until evenly mixed.
